​In science, we did end practical test about "Food Test Experiments". The aims of the experiments are investigating the use of chemical reagents to identify starch and proteins, and the presence of starch and protein in various food products. The experiments is the part of end practical test activities. Process: We did it at home and prepared all the materials independently. The teacher has given some lab tools to help us doing the experiments like test tube and the reagen (Iodine and Biuret) . We also did a research fo know what is the materials that we want to use for the experiment. The food materials I chose for the experiment is tofu, tempe, and rice. The Challenges I face during the experiment is cleaning the test tube thing The skills I need in this experiment is research skills because I have to find the materials that contains thinking skills because other skills The habit I used when doing this experiment is put first think first begin with the end in mind Overall my project is pretty cool. At first i thought this kind of experiment was kind of useless because we already know what the nutritions contain on them. But on second thought these tests actually give me an idea on which cheap foods use different kind of ingredient, losing some protein or starches.
